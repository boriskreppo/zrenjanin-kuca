More about
the house

A family mansion of 380m² situated on a plot of 821m² with an ornamental garden featuring a fountain, an enclosed area with peacocks, and a gazebo with a stone barbecue — an ideal place to relax and unwind. The house consists of a ground floor and an upper floor. The facade features decorative ornamentation. The ground floor is furnished with handcrafted period furniture. On the ground floor you will find a living room with a fireplace, a grand dining room with a concert grand piano, a kitchen overlooking the garden, a master bedroom, a blue salon, a study with a leather Chesterfield suite, fireplace and home library, as well as a bathroom with a jacuzzi tub.
The interior is adorned with large oil paintings, wall and cabinet antique clocks and handmade chandeliers. The upper floor is furnished with unique artistic furniture by a renowned Serbian sculptor. On the upper floor there is a large living room with access to two terraces overlooking the garden. Two bedrooms, a dressing room, a kitchenette and a bathroom with a separate room intended for a sauna. Below part of the house is a basement housing the boiler room, with boilers for solid fuel, gas and electricity. On the plot, next to the house, there is also a garage for two cars. The house is located in an attractive, quiet inner centre of Zrenjanin. The house is sold together with all its luxury and unique furniture.
